Privacy Policy

At ZomnIA, we value your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. ZomnIA is designed to operate without collecting personally identifiable data.

1. Information We Collect

The app does not request your name, email, or any personal data. The only content processed is the dream text you write or the audio you narrate. This information is used exclusively to generate interpretations through AI and is not stored on our servers.

2. Anonymous Dream Posting

The community inside ZomnIA is completely anonymous. No one can identify who posted a dream. All posts are automatically moderated to prevent harmful or offensive content before appearing publicly.

3. App Permissions

ZomnIA only requests minimal permissions: microphone access to narrate dreams, and notification permission to send optional updates. We do not use camera, contacts, location, or any other additional permissions.

4. Handling of Audio and Text

When you narrate a dream, the app temporarily converts the audio to text. We do not store recordings or texts in permanent databases. Nothing is linked to identities, devices, or accounts.

5. Advertising and Premium Mode

ZomnIA uses ads to remain free. These ads may use anonymous device identifiers. You can activate premium mode to remove ads. No additional purchases or donations are available.

6. Security

We do not store sensitive data or private information. ZomnIA is designed to operate without user accounts or personal databases.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available inside the app.
